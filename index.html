MENU

Explore the wide range of pizza baked in fresh dough and vegetables. Our pizza will tempt all your excitement and senses. Made with a delicate balance of everything cheesy and spicy, you will get to savour the best pizza in town.

PEPPERONI PIZZA

Savor the classic delight of perfectly baked crust topped with zesty tomato sauce, melted mozzarella, and generous slices of spicy pepperoni. A timeless favorite for every pizza lover!

CHICKEN PIZZA

Treat yourself to a flavorful feast with tender, juicy chicken chunks, zesty tomato sauce, and melted mozzarella cheese. A perfect blend of savory goodness in every bite!

GARLIC BREAD

Enjoy the irresistible aroma of freshly baked garlic bread, perfectly crisp on the outside and soft on the inside. Topped with buttery garlic and herbs, it’s a classic side you can’t resist!

PANEER CORN BREAD

Delight in the fusion of soft paneer, sweet corn, and a hint of spices on perfectly baked bread. A flavorful twist that’s crispy, cheesy, and utterly satisfying!

MIX SALAD

Savor the freshness of crisp cabbage, juicy tomatoes, crunchy onions, and tangy olives, paired with soft paneer cubes. A wholesome and refreshing salad packed with vibrant flavors!

CHOCOLATE SHAKE

Indulge in the rich and creamy goodness of our chocolate shake, made with velvety chocolate, chilled milk, and a touch of sweetness. A perfect treat for every chocolate lover!
